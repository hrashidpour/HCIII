Human-Centered Immersive Interaction & Interfaces Lab (HC^III)

A research lab focused on extended reality (XR), human–computer interaction, and 3D user interfaces, designing immersive and intelligent systems that blend seamlessly with the physical world.

We explore how VR, AR, and mixed reality can support human-centered collaboration, public safety, medicine, cyber arts, and cybersecurity through carefully designed spatial interfaces.

HC^III Framework

HC^III captures the key dimensions of the lab's work in extended reality and human–AI interaction.

  • Human-Centered UX, UI, HCI, and design thinking.
  • Immersive VR, AR, and XR experiences.
  • Intelligent AI-driven, adaptive, and context-aware interactive systems.
  • Interactions Interfaces, 3D UIs, and spatial interactions.
