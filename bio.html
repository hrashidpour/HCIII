Biography

Jeronimo Grandi

Dr. Jeronimo Grandi is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer & Cyber Sciences at Augusta University. His research focuses on extended reality (XR) and human–computer interaction (HCI), with projects spanning collaborative interactions, public safety, medicine, cyber arts, and cybersecurity.

Across this diverse application space, his work aims to design and evaluate collaborative, highly efficient spatial user interfaces that integrate naturally into the physical environment and support rich human–AI interaction.
