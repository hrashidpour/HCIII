About the Lab

The HC^III Lab explores how extended reality (XR) technologies — including virtual reality, augmented reality, and mixed reality — can support human-centered applications in collaboration, public safety, medicine, cyber arts, and cybersecurity.

We design, prototype, and evaluate spatial user interfaces that seamlessly blend digital content with the physical world, focusing on usability, user experience, and human–AI interaction.

HC^III Framework

  • Human-Centered UX, UI, HCI, and design thinking.
  • Immersive VR, AR, XR experiences for rich presence.
  • Intelligent AI-driven, adaptive, and context-aware systems.
  • Interactions Interfaces, 3DUI, and "cubed" spatial interaction metaphors.
